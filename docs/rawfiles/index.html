RawFiles: Your Private Media Gallery for Backblaze B2

Browse your Backblaze B2 cloud storage like a native photo and video gallery—with complete privacy and at a fraction of the cost.

The Problem with Today's Cloud Storage​

Most cloud services treat your files as data to be analyzed, indexed, and monetized. When you upload photos or videos to services like Google Photos, iCloud, or Dropbox:

  • Your identity is attached to every file—metadata is collected and often shared across backend services
  • You pay premium prices for storage that's bundled with features you may not need
  • You're locked into ecosystems that prioritize vendor lock-in over user freedom

Backblaze B2 offers a compelling alternative: simple, affordable object storage without the surveillance. But using B2 to browse media files feels like using a command-line tool when you want a visual gallery.

The Solution: RawFiles​

RawFiles bridges the gap between Backblaze B2's powerful, affordable storage and the intuitive browsing experience you expect from a media gallery.

🔒 Privacy-First Design​

Your files stay between your phone and B2—nothing in between.

Unlike other cloud apps that route your data through multiple backend services for processing, indexing, and analytics, RawFiles follows a simple principle:

  • No backend processing—files are streamed directly from B2 to your device
  • No metadata harvesting—we don't analyze your photos or build profiles
  • No third-party tracking—your browsing habits remain private

Your media is yours alone. RawFiles is just a window to your B2 storage—not a data collection platform.

📱 Gallery Experience for Object Storage​

B2 wasn't designed for browsing photos—RawFiles fixes that.

Backblaze B2 is a general-purpose object storage system. While excellent for backups and data archiving, it lacks:

  • Visual thumbnails for images and videos
  • Smooth scrolling through media collections
  • Intuitive organization by date, folder, or file type
  • Quick previews without downloading entire files

RawFiles transforms your B2 buckets into a beautiful, responsive gallery that feels native to your Android device.

How It Works​

  1. Connect your B2 account—just provide your application key and bucket name
  2. Browse instantly—see thumbnails of all images and videos in your bucket
  3. View full-screen—tap any media to view it in high quality
  4. Download when needed—save files locally with one tap
  5. Stay in control—your credentials never leave your device

No complex setup, no intermediate servers, no data processing pipelines. Just your phone talking directly to B2.

Get Started in Minutes​

  1. Sign up for Backblaze B2 (if you haven't already)—first 10GB is free
  2. Create a bucket for your media files
  3. Generate an application key with read access
  4. Install RawFiles from the Google Play Store
  5. Enter your credentials and start browsing

That's it! No monthly subscriptions (beyond B2 storage costs), no hidden fees, no feature limitations.

Questions?​

How secure is RawFiles?
Your B2 application keys are stored securely on your device using Android's credential encryption. We never transmit them to our servers (we don't have any servers!).

Can I use multiple B2 buckets?
Yes! Add as many buckets as you need and switch between them easily.

What file types are supported?
All common image formats (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, HEIC) and video formats (MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WebM).

Is there an iOS version?
Not yet—we're focused on delivering the best Android experience first.

What if I need help?
Contact us through the app's support section. We're a small team, but we respond to every query.
